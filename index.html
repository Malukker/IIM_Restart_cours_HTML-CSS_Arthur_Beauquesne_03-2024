Started as an high-school underground band, Avenged Sevenfold, founded in Huntington Beach, California, has long since managed to climb the highest summits of the metal universe. Earning many awards, the group has always varied its style, starting as mostly metalcore, being most often tagged as heavy metal, punk, maybe thrash metal or gothic, and honestly not caring much about it, playing for the love of music before anything else.
